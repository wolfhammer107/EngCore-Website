### **STEP 2: Google Search Console (15 minutes)**

**This is THE most important step!**

1. **Go to:** https://search.google.com/search-console

2. **Sign in** with your Google account

3. **Add property:**
   - Click "Add property"
   - Select "URL prefix"
   - Enter: `https://engcoresolutions.com.au`
   - Click "Continue"

4. **Verify ownership** (choose one method):

   **Method A: HTML Tag (Easiest)**
   - Google gives you a meta tag like: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="ABC123...">`
   - Copy it
   - Add to your index.html `<head>` section (I can help with this)
   - Upload updated index.html to GitHub
   - Go back to Search Console and click "Verify"

   **Method B: DNS Record (Alternative)**
   - Google gives you a TXT record
   - Go to Namecheap → Advanced DNS
   - Add TXT record with value Google provided
   - Click "Verify" in Search Console

5. **Submit Sitemap:**
   - Once verified, go to "Sitemaps" in left menu
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
   - ✅ Google will start crawling your site!

6. **Request Indexing:**
   - Go to "URL Inspection" (top bar)
   - Enter: `https://engcoresolutions.com.au`
   - Click "Request Indexing"
   - ✅ Tells Google to crawl ASAP (usually within 24-48 hours)
