This tells Google your site exists!

1. **Go to:** https://search.google.com/search-console

2. **Click:** "Start now" or "Add property"

3. **Enter:** `https://engcoresolutions.com.au` → Continue

4. **Verify Ownership - Choose ONE method:**

   **OPTION A: HTML Tag (Easiest)**
   - Google shows: `<meta name="google-site-verification" content="ABC123...">`
   - **STOP - Ask me to add this to your HTML** (I'll do it in 30 seconds)
   - After I add it, upload to GitHub
   - Click "Verify" in Search Console
   
   **OPTION B: DNS (Alternative)**
   - Google shows TXT record
   - Go to Namecheap → Domain → Advanced DNS
   - Add New Record → TXT Record
   - Host: `@`
   - Value: [what Google gave you]
   - Save → Click "Verify" in Search Console

5. **After Verified - Submit Sitemap:**
   - Left menu → "Sitemaps"
   - Enter: `sitemap.xml`
   - Click "Submit"
   - ✅ Google will crawl your site!

6. **Request Indexing:**
   - Top bar → "URL Inspection"
   - Enter: `https://engcoresolutions.com.au`
   - Click "Request Indexing"
   - ✅ Google prioritizes your site!
